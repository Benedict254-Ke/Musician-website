About Lulu B

Lulu B (Benedict Wambua) is a Kenyan love song artist blending heartfelt Swahili lyrics with melodic storytelling. Born on May 10, 2003 in Machakos, Kenya, he is also a web developer and coder, with a passion for music, creativity, and digital storytelling.
